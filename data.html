Our Data Sources

TrafficInsight utilizes high-quality, publicly available datasets from dataMontgomery, the official open data portal for Montgomery County, Maryland. These datasets enable us to create meaningful visualizations that provide insights into traffic patterns, safety concerns, and accident trends.

Comprehensive records of traffic accidents across Montgomery County based on data collected from the Automated Crash Reporting System by local and state police departments.
